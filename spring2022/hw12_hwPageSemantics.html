4D. Interpreting Tabbed Paragraphs

The semantics of a tabbed paragraph seems to denote that the text block being indented is an extension of the previous, unindented block of text. It can be used to add further info onto the unindented paragraph or to further explain it.

5. Interpreting <b> tags

The <b> tag is properly used on the davidmholmes.net website. The semantics of a bolded section of text seems to highlight important keywords in a paragraph or group of text. It may also be used to highlight the main point of a paragraph.
